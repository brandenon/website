A bit about Branden

Branden On is an Ottawa-born, Montreal-based multimedia artist. He primarily writes poetry and short fiction, performs music, creates short films, draws terrible comics, and likes to explore new forms whenever possible. His works primarily aim to push the boundaries of perception and interpretation. They range from joyful and naive to apathetic and dark. He is in his first year of the Communication Studies program at Concordia University.
